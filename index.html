Game Overview

Survive 7 days in a zombie-infested world. Each day brings new challenges and enemies.

Basic Rules

  • You start with 7 HP and 3 AP (Action Points) per turn
  • Draw 3 cards at the start of your turn
  • Use cards to attack enemies, heal, or gain defense
  • Defeat enemies to gain rewards and progress to the next day

Enemy Types

  • Special Enemies: Have unique effects (like exploding when defeated)
  • Boss: Appears on day 7 - the final challenge!

Tips

  • Manage your AP carefully - each card costs AP to play
  • Build up your defense before taking on stronger enemies
  • Watch out for special enemy effects!
